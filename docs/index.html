Desktop TV Remote

This application allows you to control TV by your computer.

Windows [*.zip]

Change your Android phone in IR emiter and connect it to your computer by WiFi. Now you can control your TV from desktop using keyboard shortcuts. You can set any shortcut, e.g CTRL+P turns ON/OFF your TV or other device, CTRL+UP/DOWN may be used for switching channels.

Remember that this application will work only on devices that have a built-in infrared blaster.
